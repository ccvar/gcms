Buyer FAQ

Questions usually resolved before a firm quotation is issued.

Can you run our product before order?

Yes. Send product and film samples with the target output. We issue a short test record and video before the configuration is frozen.

Which quotation term is standard?

Indicative prices are EXW Wuxi. FOB Shanghai, CIF and DAP can be quoted after the destination and packing requirements are confirmed.

What is included in FAT?

Safety interlocks, recipe changeover, output, seal quality and agreed reject logic are checked against the signed FAT protocol.

How are service and spare parts handled?

Remote commissioning is standard. On-site service, critical spare kits and regional voltage documentation are quoted separately.

Ready to freeze the specification?

Send the product, pack size, target output and destination. Engineering replies within one working day.

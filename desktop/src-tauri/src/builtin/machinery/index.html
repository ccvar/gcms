SEC 02 / CAPABILITY

Why the numbers on our tables hold

A spec table is only worth what the workshop behind it can repeat. These four things are done in-house, in our 26,000 m² plant in Xinwu District — they are the reason the same model ships with the same numbers, order after order.

2.1

Frames machined here, not bought in

Six vertical machining centres and two fibre lasers cut every frame and jaw carrier in this building. Mating faces are held to ±0.05 mm — which is where cheap machines start to rattle after six months.

6 VMC · 2 fibre lasers · ±0.05 mm

2.2

Panels built to your grid, not ours

110 / 220 / 380 / 415 V, 50 or 60 Hz — the cabinet is wired for your country before FAT, with a Siemens or Schneider parts list on request. Wiring diagrams ship in the crate, on paper and PDF.

110–415 V · 50/60 Hz · UL 508A option

2.3

Your film, on the actual machine, on camera

Send two reels of your film and 5 kg of product. We run them on the machine you would buy, film the run, and send the footage with the quotation — before you have paid anything.

2 reels + 5 kg · test filmed · free

2.4

Spares on the shelf for 10 years

A wear-parts kit rides in every crate: sealing jaws, belts, heaters, sensors. After that, stocked parts leave our warehouse within 72 hours by courier — and we guarantee availability for 10 years per model.

Kit in crate · 72 h dispatch · 10-yr guarantee

SEC 04 / FAT & SHIPPING

No machine leaves without a filmed acceptance test

Every unit runs a full factory acceptance test with your product and your film. You watch it live or on video, you sign the report — and only then is the balance due.

  1. FAT-01

    Dry run

    24 h continuous

    Full-speed empty run, logged by the PLC. Bearings, jaws and film servos are checked against the log before your product goes anywhere near the machine.

  2. FAT-02

    Your product run

    2 h at rated speed

    Your film, your product, the exact machine in your order. Seal integrity, weight scatter and reject rate are recorded on the FAT report form.

  3. FAT-03

    Live video acceptance

    60–90 min call

    A video call at your hour, camera on the machine. You direct the test, we run it. The signed FAT report — not our say-so — releases the balance payment.

  4. FAT-04

    Crate & documents

    Seaworthy export crate

    Vacuum-sealed, desiccant-packed plywood crate. Inside: CE file, wiring diagrams, wear-parts kit, and the same FAT report you signed.

Payment that follows the test: 30% deposit · 70% after the signed FAT report, before shipment. Book a film test first

What makes a quote fast

Roughly a third of the RFQs we receive need a follow-up email before we can price anything. These four lines are usually what is missing.

  1. Quantity per colourway, not just a total. 2,000 pcs in one colour and 2,000 across five are different prices.
  2. Your market. EU, US and Australia pull different test standards, and the testing is part of the cost.
  3. Packaging. Our white box, your printed colour box, or FBA-ready? It can move the unit price by more than a dollar.
  4. Delivery window. If it lands in peak season we will tell you now rather than in month two.

Trade FAQ

The six questions every buyer asks first

Answered here so you do not have to spend an email finding out.

What is your MOQ?

500 pcs per model per colourway for fabric goods, 300 pcs for sewn bags and tents, and 200 pcs for rotomoulded coolers. The count is per colourway because dye lots do not split — two colours at 250 pcs each is not 500 pcs, it is two orders below MOQ.

Below MOQ we can still quote from stock fabric colours at a 12–18% surcharge, which is usually the right way to run a market test before you commit.

Can I get samples, and what do they cost?

Yes. Existing models in stock colours ship in 5–7 days. Custom samples — your colour, your logo, your packaging — take 12 days.

Sample fees run USD 60–180 depending on the model, and are credited in full against your first production order at or above MOQ. Courier is collect on your DHL/FedEx account, or we prepay and add it to the proforma.

What are your payment terms?

30% T/T deposit, 70% against a copy of the B/L. Irrevocable L/C at sight is accepted for orders of USD 50,000 and above, subject to the issuing bank being acceptable to ours.

After three completed orders we can move to 100% T/T, 30 days after B/L. We do not accept PayPal or Western Union for production orders — if a supplier asks you to, that is worth a second thought.

What is the lead time, really?

35 days for a repeat order, 50 days when new tooling is involved. The clock starts when the deposit lands and artwork is approved — whichever comes second. Add 10 days in peak season, September to February.

Our on-time shipment rate over the last 24 months is 96.4%. When we are late through our own fault, we credit 1% of order value per week, written into the contract rather than promised in an email.

Can we audit the factory or send an inspector?

Yes, and we would rather you did. We hold a BSCI audit (last completed 2025) and welcome customer audits and third-party inspections — SGS, BV, Intertek and TÜV are all regulars here — with 5 working days notice.

If you cannot travel, we run a video walkthrough of your production run on request, and we send loading photos of every container before the doors close.

Which trade terms and ports do you work with?

FOB Ningbo is our default and our quotes assume it. EXW, CIF and DDP door are quoted on request. Ningbo-Zhoushan Port is 14 km from the plant, so drayage is same-day.

You may nominate your own forwarder; most customers do. We handle the certificate of origin, packing list and commercial invoice, and can consolidate multi-SKU orders into a single container.

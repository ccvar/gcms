Specs, MOQ & pricing

MOQ is per model, per colour

The MOQ on each card is per model. A second colourway starts its own 500 pcs count for fabric goods (dye lots), or 200 pcs for rotomoulded goods. Below MOQ we quote from stock colours at a 12–18% surcharge.

Lead time starts at two events

Deposit received and artwork approved — whichever is later. Add 10 days in peak season (September–February). New tooling adds 25 days and is quoted separately.

Prices are FOB Ningbo

Quoted in USD, valid 30 days, based on current aluminium and resin indices. EXW, CIF and DDP door available on request. Tiered pricing appears on each product page.

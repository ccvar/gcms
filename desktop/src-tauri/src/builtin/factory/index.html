Production process

From your drawing to a sealed container

Lead times below are what we write into the proforma invoice, not best-case marketing numbers.

  1. STEP 01

    Sampling

    12 days

    Send a 2D drawing, a spec sheet or a physical reference. You get a costed BOM and 1–3 samples back. Sample fee is credited in full against your first production order.

  2. STEP 02

    Tooling

    25 days

    Extrusion dies, injection moulds and sewing patterns are cut in-house. Tooling cost is refunded after 20,000 pcs. 640+ existing tools mean most projects never reach this step.

  3. STEP 03

    Mass production

    35 days

    6 assembly lines, 3.2M pcs annual capacity. Repeat orders run 35 days; add 10 days in peak season (September–February). Weekly WIP photos on request.

  4. STEP 04

    Quality control

    4 days

    IQC on incoming material, IPQC every 2 hours on the line, FQC at AQL 2.5 / 4.0. Third-party inspectors (SGS, BV, Intertek, TÜV) welcome with 5 working days notice.

  5. STEP 05

    Shipping

    7 days

    FOB Ningbo by default; EXW, CIF and DDP door quoted on request. Packing list, loading photos and test reports go out before the container doors close.

New product, from approved drawing to FOB Ningbo: ~90 days Repeat order, deposit to FOB: 42 days Start with a sample request

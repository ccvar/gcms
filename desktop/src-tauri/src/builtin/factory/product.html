Customisation

Everything below is done in-house. Nothing here is subcontracted, which is why we can quote it in days rather than weeks.

Colour

Pantone TPX/TCX match on fabric, powder coat on the frame. Lab dips in 7 days for your sign-off; we do not start bulk dyeing until you approve one. Each additional colourway carries its own MOQ because dye lots do not split.

500 pcs per colourway · lab dip 7 days

Size

Seat height adjusts from 380 to 460 mm and seat width by ±60 mm without new tooling — we simply cut different tube lengths and re-cut the fabric pattern. Anything outside that envelope needs a new extrusion die.

No tooling within 380–460 mm

Logo

Silkscreen 1–4 colours, heat transfer, woven label sewn into the seat seam, or embossed into the armrest. The first three are free at MOQ; embossing needs its own mould.

Embossing tooling USD 1,200

Packaging

White box, printed colour box, retail hangtag, EAN/UPC labelling and Amazon FBA prep including polybag and FNSKU. Send us the dieline or let our studio draw one from your brand guide.

Artwork proof in 3 days

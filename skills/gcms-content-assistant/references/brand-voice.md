# Brand Voice

## Default Voice

- Clear, practical, and specific.
- Product-minded, not hype-driven.
- Helpful to operators, founders, editors, and developers.
- Confident but not exaggerated.
- Prefer concrete benefits and workflows over abstract claims.

## Chinese Copy

- Use natural simplified Chinese.
- Keep sentences compact.
- Prefer verbs like "查看", "创建", "维护", "发布", "复核", "同步".
- Avoid empty marketing phrases such as "赋能", "极致", "颠覆", "黑科技", unless the site already uses them.
- Explain operational value: less repetitive work, clearer permissions, safer publishing, easier review.

## English Copy

- Use plain product English.
- Prefer active voice.
- Avoid inflated SaaS language.
- Keep technical claims precise.
- Use "draft", "review", "publish", "revoke", "scope", and "audit" consistently.

## When Rewriting Existing Content

- Match the existing page's tone before changing it.
- Preserve the original intent and factual claims.
- Improve clarity, structure, and SEO without changing meaning.
- Do not add unsupported numbers, customer claims, or comparisons.
